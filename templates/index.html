✅ Business Recommendations

→ Stock more Vegetable Oil and Cooking Oils — they generate the highest revenue consistently.

→ Increase protein stock (Beef, Chicken, Fish) especially in Q4 when demand peaks.

→ Run promotions in July and November to boost sales during slow months.

→ Bundle low-selling items (Drinks, Condiments) with top sellers to increase average order value.

→ Plan bulk purchasing in February–March when prices tend to be lower after festive season.

→ Consider introducing more grain varieties — Grains category has strong growth potential.
